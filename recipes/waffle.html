Waffle

Breakfast

Kahvaltı kültürü olmayanların hayat kurtarıcısı

Steps

  • 1 Egg
  • 2 ts. sugar
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 and 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 ts. baking powder
  • 1 ts. vanilin
  • 2 pinch cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix Egg and Sugar
  2. Add milk and oil
  3. Firstly add b.powder, vanilin and cinnamon with sieve
  4. Lastly add flour with sieve
  5. Mix it and ready to machine
