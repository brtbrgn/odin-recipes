Waffle

Description

Sabah sabah kahvaltı mı edilir sorusuna cevap olarak hayatıma girmiş waffle tarifim.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 2 ts. sugar
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 and 1/2 cup flour
  • a package baking powder
  • a package vanilin
  • 2 pinch cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix Egg and Sugar
  2. Add milk and oil
  3. Firstly add b.powder, vanilin and cinnamon with sieve
  4. Lastly add flour with sieve
  5. Mix it and ready to machine
